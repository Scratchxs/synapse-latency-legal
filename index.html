No Bs Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Most privacy policies are designed to bore you into submission. This one isn’t.

We’ll keep it simple: our games collect minimal gameplay related data to improve your experience. No personal details, no invasive tracking, just enough to make sure the world or space reacts to your choices in meaningful ways.

By continuing to use any of our titles, you agree to this policy. If that sounds ominous, relax, we are not selling your data to shady third parties.

Now, let’s break this down properly.

2. What Data We Collect

When you interact with our games, we may store the following information:

  • Anonymized Player ID: a random identifier generated on your device so the system can tell one unique player from another. This contains no personal details.
  • Your Choices: decisions may be logged in anonymized form to track global player trends and improve narrative branching.
  • Total Player Count: we track how many active players exist to monitor engagement.
  • Notification Settings: whether you have notifications enabled, so we know if we should send alerts or leave you drifting in silence.

What We Do Not Collect:

  • Your real name, email, or address.
  • Your browsing history, private messages, or personal files.
  • Any biometric, financial, or legally sensitive data.

The goal is to enhance gameplay, not monitor your personal life.

3. Where Data is Stored and Who Has Access

All data is securely stored and handled through:

  • Cloudflare: manages security and server protection.
  • Anthem Syndicate: the development team maintaining the backend.

No third party advertisers, no data reselling, no unauthorized access.

4. What We Actually Do With This Data

  • Choice statistics help us understand how players interact with the story.
  • Player count lets us measure engagement, server load, and whether anyone is still out there listening.
  • Notification settings ensure the system respects your preferences.

That’s it. No behavioral profiling, no targeted ads, and no secret experiments in the dark void.

However, if we notice that 98% of players make a logical choice while 2% consistently select the worst possible option, we might laugh at the statistics. We will not know who made the choice, but we will laugh at it.

5. Your Rights

Because all stored data is anonymous, we cannot identify individual players. This means:

  • No Personal Data: we do not store names, emails, or anything that could link back to you directly.
  • Anonymized IDs: each player is assigned a random identifier so the system can tell one unique user from another. These IDs contain no personal details.
  • Deletion Requests: since we cannot know which anonymized ID belongs to you, we cannot directly fulfill deletion requests. You can reset your game data locally at any time, which generates a new anonymized ID and stops any further events from being associated with the old one.

If you live in a region with strict data protection laws, such as GDPR or CCPA, this setup already minimizes your exposure, because nothing we store can be tied back to you personally.

6. Data Retention

  • Gameplay statistics and anonymized IDs are kept as long as they remain useful for analytics and system stability.
  • We periodically clear out redundant or inactive records to keep the database lean.
  • Settings stored on your device, such as notifications, remain only until you reset or uninstall the game.

We keep what is needed to improve the experience, and nothing more. When data stops being useful, it is gone.

7. Policy Updates

If we change this policy, we will announce it in an in game update log and update the date at the top of this page.

If you continue using our games after an update, you accept the revised policy. If you disagree, you can stop using the game and reset your local data at any time.

8. Contact Information

For privacy questions or complaints, contact:

Email: definitelyhuman@anthem-syndicate.com

We actually read messages, so if you need assistance, reach out.

Your data is yours. We are here to ensure our games remain as immersive and unsettling as the choices you make in the dark.

Now get back to it, someone, somewhere, is waiting on your next move.
